Dream World I

About

In this game you find yourself in a wacky dream world filled with loads of people to meet and places to explore. Your main objective is to find a way out of the dream.

Importance

As this is my first real Roblox game, it has loads of achievements. For starters, this game was my first Roblox game where I actually programmed stuff. This game was also my first time using a real programming language (that being Luau).
