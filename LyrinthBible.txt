
Lyrinth Bible

1. Creation
In an unknown time a god all knowing and powerfull sought to create a world which it could watch. From itself it created the planet known as Lyrinth. It covered Lyrinth with water and dotted it with land. The biggest of these land masses would be named by its inhabitants as "Lyrinth".
The god then created a ball of eternal fire known as the "sun" and placed it at the center. Lyrinth was the gods most prized creation yet it was unsatisfied. Lyrinth was unmoving and unchanging. The god created the moon to pull the sea apart creating waves that would breath life into the land. Changing it ever so slightly.
Despite this the god remained unsatisfied and devised another solution. From itself it created the spirits. Each with its own unique purpose. These spirits would reside in the spirit realm a place where the spirits could influence the gods creation. With their new task the spirits began to work.

2. The forest spirit was amoung the first to influence the land. From itself it shed seeds of all kinds. Populating the land with all sort of flora. The forest spirit also created many unqiue beigns known as amorians. Each amorian was different some where small, other large. Some had wings, some had claws, some had none at all.
Unlike the fona the amorians where granted the abilty to move and interact with their environment. Amorians however lacked the abilty to think simply acting on survival instinct. For every action the spirit did onto the physical realm a small portion of itself was transfered into the physical realm. This meant that the spirits could forever create but the power which they could instill into their creations would increasingly dwindle.
Fona and amorians drew little power from the spirits and thus had relativly little affect on the land. After the world was populated with lesser beigns the spirits began to create new more powerfull beigns.

3. The wizzards where created by the spirits and contained significant power to alter the land. This power steamed from two things the first being their ability to have self thought and action and the second being their ability to channel in the physical realm. The wizzards while gifted with freedom where also given the task of enacting the will of their respective spirits. This task was in equal power to their free will. With the spirits power mostly utilized they became dormant watching and guiding their wizzards.
With thought came the first era known as the Ancient Era. When the wizzards first sprouted from the land they where weak and deffence less. The wizzards learned to feed off of the fona and amorians but so did some of the amorians. Not long after the first wizzards came the "invention" of magic (channeling in the physical realm). With magic the wizzards where able to split their own power and utilize it to enact change in the physical realm. Most wizards used their own power to channel but some stronger ones found other sources of power to draw from.
With this magic the wizzards began to alter the land. Despite the wizzards magic they where still mortal. The more vigorus and frequent they channeled the faster they would run out of their power (all be it over the course of hundreds of years). Trying to preserve their own power while also carrying out the will of their spirit the wizzards began to experiment with ways to achieve both goals at the same time.
Eventually they discovered that they could draw power from their spirits which could be used to channel with. This spirit power could not be used to channel something of greater power than the wizzard itself. Some wizzards drew dirrectly from their spirit creating halflings. Halflings where sparse as it required powerfull wizzards to draw exclusivly from the spirit. What was more common where quartlings which where drawn by both the power of the wizzard and the power of the spirit significantly decreaing the wizzards power usage.
